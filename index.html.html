Automatic Tap Opener System

Detection: When the sensor detects the presence of a person’s hands (using an IR sensor or ultrasonic sensor), it sends a signal to the Arduino. Signal Processing: The Arduino processes the sensor’s data and, based on the condition set (e.g., if motion is detected), it triggers the relay. Action: The relay or transistor then sends a signal to the solenoid valve or motorized mechanism connected to the tap, opening the valve and allowing water to flow. Water Flow Control: After a predefined period or when no motion is detected (using a timer or another sensor), the system will automatically close the tap to prevent water wastage.
